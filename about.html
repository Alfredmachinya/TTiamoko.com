About TiaMoko Entrepreneurship & Leadership Centre

At TiaMoko Entrepreneurship & Leadership Centre, we are dedicated to fostering the next generation of entrepreneurs and leaders. Our mission is to empower individuals with the skills, knowledge, and confidence they need to make a positive impact in their communities and beyond.

Founded in 2025, our centre has been at the forefront of entrepreneurial education and leadership training. We offer a range of programs and workshops designed to meet the needs of aspiring entrepreneurs, business owners, and leaders at all stages of their journey.

Our core values are integrity, innovation, and inclusivity. We believe that by nurturing a diverse and inclusive environment, we can inspire creativity and drive positive change.

Join us at TiaMoko Entrepreneurship & Leadership Centre and be part of a vibrant community committed to excellence and growth. Together, we can build a brighter future.
